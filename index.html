The Face of Water

Summary

In The Face of Water, Sarah Ruden brilliantly and elegantly explains and celebrates the Bible’s writings. Singling out the most famous passages, such as the Genesis creation story, the Ten Commandments, the Lord’s Prayer, and the Beatitudes, Ruden reexamines and retranslates from the Hebrew and Greek what has been obscured and misunderstood over time. “A meaty work that will delight both those who are fascinated by language, and those who care deeply about the Bible and how we read and interpret it.” — C. Christopher Smith, RELEVANT Magazine Learn More.
